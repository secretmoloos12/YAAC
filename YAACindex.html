YAAC ( Young Adult Advisory Club ) is a Youth Organization in Kosovo Prizren.
We want to keep the Nature clean, and also have fun.
Young Adult Advisory Club aims to bring people together through activities that help our community.
Pliking, or picking up trash while hiking , is a keystone activity the club does in Marash.
We believe one of the things that makes Prizren a great city is the close by nature of Marash.
We enjoy walking through and picking up the trash we can . We believe that access to nature improves quality of life for citizens and it is fun to get together.
Other activities involve making and handing out kindness cards. This arts and craft is fun and helps spread positivity in the city.
Other ideas in the making are letter writing, baking dog treats for homeless dogs, and reading to the olderly.
The club meets at American Corner every other Friday at 1 PM ( 13:00 ).
